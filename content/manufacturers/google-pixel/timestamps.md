## Time Zone Considerations

- Filename typically uses local time zone
- EXIF DateTimeOriginal should match
- GPS timestamp in UTC (if present)
- Mismatch may indicate:
  - Travel/time zone change
  - Manual clock adjustment
  - File manipulation

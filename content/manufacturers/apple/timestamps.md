## Timestamp Variations

### Time Zone Handling
- Usually local time zone
- No standard for UTC vs local
- Varies by manufacturer

### Precision
- Most: Second precision (HHMMSS)
- Some: Millisecond precision (HHMMSS###)
- Older: Minute precision only
